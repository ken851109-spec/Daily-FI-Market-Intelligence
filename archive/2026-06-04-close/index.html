投資解讀

盤面定義

一、UST 收盤與 Curve 變化

UST 是前端與中段回落、長端仍黏著的 mild 轉陡。

美債收盤的盤面定義是前端 / 中段回落，而不是乾淨的長端偏多收斂。2Y 收 4.05%、日降 3bp，5Y 收 4.18%、日降 3bp，10Y 收 4.47%、日降 2bp，20Y 收 4.98%、30Y 收 4.97%，兩端都回落但前端與中段更明顯。2s10s 小幅轉陡至 42bp、5s30s 至 79bp，代表市場先把申請失業救濟與油價回落反映到政策敏感段，長端期限溢價仍沒有完全退場。

官方 CMT 的周/月變化顯示，這次回落只是把前一日的升息溢價修正一部分，尚未改變整個月的高利率背景。2Y 一周仍升 7bp、月升 12bp，5Y 一周升 5bp、月升 10bp，10Y 一周升 2bp、月升 4bp；30Y 一周低 2bp、月低 1bp。Curve 上 2s10s 一周仍收斂 5bp、月內收斂 8bp，配置不宜把一天的回落誤解成 Fed 已重新回到降息路徑。

Jobless 初領失業金是本次利率回落的第一個數據觸發。上周初領失業救濟升 13k 至 225k，高於 211k 預期與 212k 前值，為 2 月以來高點；四周均值升至 214.75k，續領降至 178 萬。這組數字沒有顯示 layoffs 失控，但足以提醒市場勞動市場正在低招聘、低裁員狀態中鬆動，前端不再能只依 ADP +122k 與 ISM Services 54.5 來定價升息。

油價回落是第二個觸發。WTI 估計收在 92.50 美元附近、日跌約 2.9%，Brent 盤中回到 96.40 美元附近，因投資人押注美伊談判與以黎停火仍有進展。能源風險溢價下降會先壓低 CPI pass-through 與 Fed 的二階效應擔憂，因此 2Y-5Y 比 30Y 更容易反彈；但只要 Strait 相關不確定未完全消除，10Y 下方 4.45% 仍不應視為無風險突破。

跨資產收盤支持這個解讀。S&P 500 上漲 0.4% 至 7,584.31，Dow 上漲 1.7% 至 51,561.93 並創高，Nasdaq 小跌 0.1% 至 26,830.96，反映市場從晶片股轉向金融、小型股與傳統景氣股。股市廣度改善同時伴隨 UST 反彈，代表今天不是衰退恐慌買債，而是油價與前端政策溢價降溫後的跨資產 rebalancing。

長端的訊號仍偏黏著。20Y 4.98%、30Y 4.97%，10s30s 維持 50bp，說明長端沒有隨 2Y 與 5Y 同幅下行；MBS convexity、AI 資本支出、IG 供給與財政赤字仍壓在長端。若 10Y 要有效跌破 4.45%，需要非農與工資共同確認需求降溫，而不只是初領失業救濟單週高於預期。

供給與 microstructure 也開始進入利率判讀。美國 IG 發行今年已達 1 兆美元、速度為 2020 年以來最快，Apollo 認為全年 IG 銷售可能超過美債淨供給；同時 5% 以上票息的 MBS 存量超過 2 兆美元，約三分之一 outstanding MBS 接近 par。這些因素不一定主導每日收盤，但會讓 30Y 對每次 selloff 更敏感，並限制 Curve 回落的延展性。

定價邏輯

二、美債走勢解讀

定價邏輯從升息尾端過熱修正，轉為等待非農確認的雙向 Fed path。

這次美債反彈的本質是升息尾端風險降溫，而不是 Fed 政策反應函數完全轉鴿。Daly 表示政策位置合適但需要保留雙向反應，Logan 仍提醒今年可能需要升息，Morgan Stanley 則認為 Fed 若思考升息，可能不會只因戰爭推高能源價格就行動，而會更重視核心通膨、財政、銀行貸款與 AI spending。這使前端從單向 hawkish 重定價回到 data-dependent range。

Claims 225k 高於預期，直接打開市場對周五非農的疑問。若非農接近 +85k 共識、失業率維持 4.3%、平均時薪 +0.3% m/m，2Y 4.05% 可能短線守住；若就業大幅高於 +125k 或薪資重新升溫，今天降下來的 2Y-5Y 會被快速重定價。Curve 因此不宜稱作持久偏多轉陡，而是非農就業前的風險降低。

油價傳導也變得更細緻。WTI 跌近 3% 確實降低了 immediate 通膨溢價，但 Fed 官員仍需面對 April PCE / CPI 一類價格指標在 3.8% 附近、能源成本仍比戰前高、企業包材與運輸成本尚未完全回落。只要油價停在 90 美元上方，Fed 就很難把所有衝擊視為一次性供給事件；前端可以反彈，但不應把年底升息可能性歸零。

長端壓力來自 funding，而不是單純 macro。Apollo 指出 AI、國防、公用事業與資料中心需要龐大長天期資本，美國 IG 供給已達 1 兆美元；Schneider Electric 借 AI 資料中心 story 發行 8 億歐元 convertible，同時回購 2030 年債。當企業資本需求與 Treasury cash need 同時偏高，30Y 4.97% 的絕對收益會吸引買盤，但期限溢價很難快速壓回低位。

MBS convexity 是長端波動的放大器。高票息 MBS 重新接近 par，使投資人需要更頻繁調整 hedge；Goldman 估算近期 selloff 使 active mortgage hedgers 的利率曝險變化相當於 400 億美元 10Y Treasury。這代表若 10Y 重新上破 4.55%，selloff 可能被 mortgage rebalancing 放大；若 10Y 跌破 4.45%，同一機制也可能短線放大反彈。

美元與黃金的組合顯示市場尚未切換到全面避險。DXY 回落至 99.22、黃金升至 4,470 美元，但 S&P 500 與 Dow 同時上漲，表示投資人把油價回落與利率下行視為 risk broadening，而不是衰退避險。對 FI 來說，這種組合對 5Y-10Y 有利，卻不一定利多超長端，因為風險資產仍吸收資金。

因此本次定價邏輯應被描述為 mild 轉陡 with 偏黏著長端 premium。前端與中段受益於初領失業金與油價，長端受限於供給、MBS convexity、BOJ normalization 與 AI funding。若未來幾日 2s10s 只在 40-45bp 區間震盪，代表市場仍在等待非農與 6 月 FOMC；若 Curve 快速打開，才表示 Fed cut tail 或期限溢價有一方明顯改變。

跨市驗證

三、其他市場動向

跨市驗證顯示央行分化、亞洲 FX 防守、信用供給與 AI funding 同時重塑風險定價。

日本是全球央行分化的核心。實質薪資年增 1.9%、高於 1.7% 預期並連續第四個月為正，名目薪資 +3.5%、基本薪資 +3.4%，都支持 BOJ 6/15-16 升息 25bp 的討論；但 household spending 實質年減 0.5%，連五個月下降。這個組合讓 JGB 與日圓很難單向交易，因為 wage-price loop 變強，但家計需求仍限制 BOJ 追趕油價的速度。

日本政策還有財政與補貼層。政府準備 3.1 兆日圓追加預算，可能延長汽油與電力補貼；食品與飲料業者 6 月漲價產品超過 1,000 項，遠高於 5 月的 84 項。補貼可壓低 headline，但也延後相對價格調整，對 BOJ 而言反而增加判斷 underlying inflation 的難度；對美元債投資人而言，JPY 接近 160 仍是 FX / NAV 的核心風險。

澳洲與紐西蘭提供另一種 Duration 訊號。RBA 現金利率已回到 4.35%，Bullock 表示升息正在透過住房市場發揮作用，Q1 GDP 只增 0.3%、4 月 household spending -1.1%、失業率升至四年半高點；紐西蘭 2031 債標售 bid-to-cover 達 6.22，為該券 2020 年以來最高。這些市場顯示，若成長放緩與供給削減同時出現，中段 Duration 仍能吸引真實需求。

歐洲不是同步升息。Czech CPI 5 月 2.1%，低於 2.3% 預期且符合央行預測，服務價格 4.7% 仍高但降低立即升息壓力；Sweden CPIF ex-energy +0.5%，高於市場 0.3% 但低於 Riksbank 0.9% 預測，headline 1.5% 接近央行路徑。這代表歐洲 rates trade 會更依賴各國核心、服務與 FX，而不是只跟著 ECB 或油價走。

亞洲 FX 防守仍是區域資產的主線。韓元跌到 2009 年以來低點，印尼盾觸及紀錄低位，印度盧比在 95-96 區間、接近 97 的紀錄低點；韓國、印尼、菲律賓與印度都用口頭警告、干預或資本流入措施守匯率。這會讓本地債牛市被匯率風險打斷，也使美元走勢成為 TWD 海外債 ETF 的主要跨市風險。

信用一級市場仍能開啟，但需要清楚故事與保護。Schneider Electric 以 AI 資料中心 power / cooling narrative 發行 8 億歐元可轉債，票息 0.25%-0.75%、轉換溢價 35%-40%，並回購 6.5 億歐元 2030 年債；Coastal GasLink 準備 C$1bn 兩段式高評級債，短段約政府債 +100bp、長段 +115bp。這些例子支持 IG 與 infrastructure 收益，但不代表所有 credit beta 都可追。

AI funding 的供需兩面都在放大。Whale Rock 看好 Anthropic 使用者與 enterprise 市場，SpaceX 擬籌 750 億美元並可能以 1.77 兆美元估值上市；同時 DoubleLine 提醒 AI debt 最終幾乎一定會形成 bubble，去年以來相關高級、HY、貸款與其他 debt 銷售超過 3,700 億美元。對 FI 來說，AI 是盈利與資本支出支撐，也是長端供給與信用槓桿來源。

特殊信用風險更分散。Nassau County tobacco settlement bonds 未付 3,600 萬美元 principal，成為 800 億美元 tobacco sector 的首例 default，因 2025 settlement distributions 年減 19%、美國成人吸菸率降至 9.9%；Sleep Number 可能準備 bankruptcy loan，2025 revenue 14 億美元、年減 16%，股價四個月跌約 90%。這些不是宏觀 beta，而是收入池收縮與資本結構壓力。

EM 主權仍由條款、商品與資本流主導。Zambia 可能把 2053 bond 回購提高到 85c，債券約 82.14c，若 IMF 指標達成票息將由 0.5% 升至 7.5%；Chile 追加 62 億美元借款，另有 174 億美元原授權，2032 peso bond 標售比二級市場高 12bp，2056 高 10bp。投資人會買收益，但 fiscal credibility 與重整 mechanics 決定利差。

South Africa rand 收益顯示高 real 殖利率仍能吸引資金。Rand 自 4 月以來美元 funded 收益回報約 5%，非居民買入 372.9 億 rand 政府債，10Y 殖利率降 67bp 至約 8.65%；但 SARB 上周升息 25bp 至 7%，仍提示若油價衝突持續可能再緊。這說明 EM 收益可以恢復，但需要美元回落、vol 下降與政策可信度同時支撐。

配置動作

四、配置含義

配置可回補中段 Duration，但長端、HY beta 與亞洲未避險 NAV 仍需門檻。

Duration 建議從 short-to-neutral 調回 neutral-to-slight-long，但只集中在 5Y-10Y，不追 20Y-30Y。2Y 與 5Y 日降 3bp、初領失業金 225k 高於預期、WTI 跌近 3%，使中段有基本面回落；但 30Y 仍在 4.97%，IG 供給、MBS convexity 與 AI 資本支出仍在。實作上，10Y 4.45% 附近才分批加碼，4.55% 上方則停止追價。

Curve 部位偏好 mild 轉陡的防守表達，而不是 aggressively long 長端。2s10s 42bp、5s30s 79bp，日內各轉陡 1bp，但周/月仍大幅收斂，代表政策風險尚未完全退場。若非農弱於 +50k 且薪資低於 +0.2%，可把 5Y-10Y bull steepener 放大；若非農高於 +125k，應把前端回落視為賣點。

Credit / ETF 仍偏高品質收益。IG 一級市場達 1 兆美元且高評級 AI / infrastructure 名稱能清盤，支持 short/intermediate IG；但 tobacco default、Sleep Number bankruptcy risk、Reno de Medici 債券約 26c、software / 私募信貸 stress 都提醒 HY beta 不能用 index 利差一筆帶過。配置上保留 IG、金融、utility、data-center picks-and-shovels，避免弱零售與單一收入池結構。

EM 配置可做 selective 收益，但不應放大本幣匯率風險。South Africa 有 5% 收益回報與 372.9 億 rand 債券流入，Zambia 有 tender catalyst，Congo 受銅鈷與 AI 基礎設施 narrative 支撐；相對地 Chile fiscal slippage、Asia FX intervention 與印度盧比壓力仍會放大本地債波動。EM 硬通貨優先於未避險本幣，且要避開油價再上 98 美元的情境。

TWD 計價海外債 ETF 的加碼條件比昨日起更接近，但仍需確認。10Y 4.47% 已回到可分批區間，DXY 99.22 有助 NAV，但 USD/JPY 接近 160 與亞洲 FX 防守顯示匯率成本未完全釋放。若 DXY 跌破 99、WTI 低於 92、10Y 守在 4.45%-4.50%，可把加碼從觀察轉成執行；若 DXY 重新上 100，維持小步分批。

長端配置只適合用 convexity 小比例表達。若 30Y 上破 5.05%，MBS hedging、Treasury funding 與 AI debt crowding-out 會使 selloff 放大；若 30Y 跌破 4.90% 且 10Y 同步跌破 4.45%，才代表實錢資金 demand 重新接管。退休金或保險帳戶可以保留長端 convexity，但 total-return 帳戶應把主要風險放在 5Y-10Y。

現金與短票仍有配置價值。Fed path 雖被初領失業金降溫，但 Daly、Logan 與 Warsh 首次 FOMC 都要求保留再定價空間；若周五非農就業是 Goldilocks，短端收益會慢慢被再投資壓力吃掉，若數據太強，短票反而提供保護。因此 barbell 仍合理：短端留流動性，中段收反彈，長端小比例做 hedge。

交易觸發

五、後續觀察重點

交易觸發集中在非農、10Y/30Y 關鍵位、WTI、DXY、BOJ 與信用供給。

第一個 trigger 是周五非農。若非農就業高於 +125k、失業率低於 4.3% 或 AHE 高於 +0.3% m/m，2Y 4.05% 的回落會很快消失，10Y 可能回測 4.55%；若非農就業低於 +50k、初領失業金四周均值續升，10Y 跌破 4.45% 的機率上升，5Y-10Y Duration 可以再加一段。

第二個 trigger 是 WTI 92 / 98。WTI 低於 92 美元且外交進展延續，前端通膨溢價會繼續下降；WTI 重新站上 98 美元，Fed 對 energy pass-through 的防守會回來，2s10s 可能再收斂。這也是 TWD 海外債 ETF 加碼或暫緩的直接門檻。

第三個 trigger 是 10Y 4.45% / 4.55% 與 30Y 5.05%。10Y 跌破 4.45% 且 30Y 不守在 5% 上方，代表反彈已從前端傳到長端；10Y 上破 4.55% 或 30Y 上破 5.05%，則說明非農就業、MBS hedge 或供給壓力重新主導，Duration 要降回中性。

第四個 trigger 是 DXY 99 / 100。DXY 低於 99，亞洲 FX 防守壓力會下降，TWD 計價海外債 NAV 更容易保留 UST 反彈；DXY 重新上 100，韓元、印尼盾、盧比與日圓都可能再承壓，未避險海外債與 EM 本幣債需同步降風險。

第五個 trigger 是 BOJ 6/15-16。若日本薪資資料促成 25bp hike 且 Ueda 提示 2026 還有加息空間，JGB 長端與避險成本會限制亞洲買美元債；若 BOJ 只小幅升息且強調 household spending 疲弱，日圓可能先反彈、JGB selloff 可控，對 USD Duration 反而是穩定因子。

第六個 trigger 是信用供給品質。若 IG 新券仍能縮窄、Schneider 類 AI 基礎設施 paper 被快速吸收、Coastal GasLink 高評級 supply 順利，credit 收益可保留；若 tobacco、retail、software 或私募信貸相關壓力擴散到新券 concessions，Credit / ETF beta 要比 Duration 更早降風險。

第七個 trigger 是 MBS convexity。若 10Y 接近 4.55% 時 MBS OAS 擴大、mortgage hedgers 增加 receiver-to-payer adjustment，selloff 可能被放大；若 10Y 回到 4.40%-4.45% 且 MBS 穩定，convexity flow 會反過來支撐反彈。這個 trigger 決定長端能否加入中段回落。

一、UST 收盤與 Curve 變化UST 是前端與中段回落、長端仍黏著的 mild 轉陡。

美債收盤的盤面定義是前端 / 中段回落，而不是乾淨的長端偏多收斂。2Y 收 4.05%、日降 3bp，5Y 收 4.18%、日降 3bp，10Y 收 4.47%、日降 2bp，20Y 收 4.98%、30Y 收 4.97%，兩端都回落但前端與中段更明顯。2s10s 小幅轉陡至 42bp、5s30s 至 79bp，代表市場先把申請失業救濟與油價回落反映到政策敏感段，長端期限溢價仍沒有完全退場。

官方 CMT 的周/月變化顯示，這次回落只是把前一日的升息溢價修正一部分，尚未改變整個月的高利率背景。2Y 一周仍升 7bp、月升 12bp，5Y 一周升 5bp、月升 10bp，10Y 一周升 2bp、月升 4bp；30Y 一周低 2bp、月低 1bp。Curve 上 2s10s 一周仍收斂 5bp、月內收斂 8bp，配置不宜把一天的回落誤解成 Fed 已重新回到降息路徑。

Jobless 初領失業金是本次利率回落的第一個數據觸發。上周初領失業救濟升 13k 至 225k，高於 211k 預期與 212k 前值，為 2 月以來高點；四周均值升至 214.75k，續領降至 178 萬。這組數字沒有顯示 layoffs 失控，但足以提醒市場勞動市場正在低招聘、低裁員狀態中鬆動，前端不再能只依 ADP +122k 與 ISM Services 54.5 來定價升息。

油價回落是第二個觸發。WTI 估計收在 92.50 美元附近、日跌約 2.9%，Brent 盤中回到 96.40 美元附近，因投資人押注美伊談判與以黎停火仍有進展。能源風險溢價下降會先壓低 CPI pass-through 與 Fed 的二階效應擔憂，因此 2Y-5Y 比 30Y 更容易反彈；但只要 Strait 相關不確定未完全消除，10Y 下方 4.45% 仍不應視為無風險突破。

跨資產收盤支持這個解讀。S&P 500 上漲 0.4% 至 7,584.31，Dow 上漲 1.7% 至 51,561.93 並創高，Nasdaq 小跌 0.1% 至 26,830.96，反映市場從晶片股轉向金融、小型股與傳統景氣股。股市廣度改善同時伴隨 UST 反彈，代表今天不是衰退恐慌買債，而是油價與前端政策溢價降溫後的跨資產 rebalancing。

長端的訊號仍偏黏著。20Y 4.98%、30Y 4.97%，10s30s 維持 50bp，說明長端沒有隨 2Y 與 5Y 同幅下行；MBS convexity、AI 資本支出、IG 供給與財政赤字仍壓在長端。若 10Y 要有效跌破 4.45%，需要非農與工資共同確認需求降溫，而不只是初領失業救濟單週高於預期。

供給與 microstructure 也開始進入利率判讀。美國 IG 發行今年已達 1 兆美元、速度為 2020 年以來最快，Apollo 認為全年 IG 銷售可能超過美債淨供給；同時 5% 以上票息的 MBS 存量超過 2 兆美元，約三分之一 outstanding MBS 接近 par。這些因素不一定主導每日收盤，但會讓 30Y 對每次 selloff 更敏感，並限制 Curve 回落的延展性。

二、美債走勢解讀定價邏輯從升息尾端過熱修正，轉為等待非農確認的雙向 Fed path。

這次美債反彈的本質是升息尾端風險降溫，而不是 Fed 政策反應函數完全轉鴿。Daly 表示政策位置合適但需要保留雙向反應，Logan 仍提醒今年可能需要升息，Morgan Stanley 則認為 Fed 若思考升息，可能不會只因戰爭推高能源價格就行動，而會更重視核心通膨、財政、銀行貸款與 AI spending。這使前端從單向 hawkish 重定價回到 data-dependent range。

Claims 225k 高於預期，直接打開市場對周五非農的疑問。若非農接近 +85k 共識、失業率維持 4.3%、平均時薪 +0.3% m/m，2Y 4.05% 可能短線守住；若就業大幅高於 +125k 或薪資重新升溫，今天降下來的 2Y-5Y 會被快速重定價。Curve 因此不宜稱作持久偏多轉陡，而是非農就業前的風險降低。

油價傳導也變得更細緻。WTI 跌近 3% 確實降低了 immediate 通膨溢價，但 Fed 官員仍需面對 April PCE / CPI 一類價格指標在 3.8% 附近、能源成本仍比戰前高、企業包材與運輸成本尚未完全回落。只要油價停在 90 美元上方，Fed 就很難把所有衝擊視為一次性供給事件；前端可以反彈，但不應把年底升息可能性歸零。

長端壓力來自 funding，而不是單純 macro。Apollo 指出 AI、國防、公用事業與資料中心需要龐大長天期資本，美國 IG 供給已達 1 兆美元；Schneider Electric 借 AI 資料中心 story 發行 8 億歐元 convertible，同時回購 2030 年債。當企業資本需求與 Treasury cash need 同時偏高，30Y 4.97% 的絕對收益會吸引買盤，但期限溢價很難快速壓回低位。

MBS convexity 是長端波動的放大器。高票息 MBS 重新接近 par，使投資人需要更頻繁調整 hedge；Goldman 估算近期 selloff 使 active mortgage hedgers 的利率曝險變化相當於 400 億美元 10Y Treasury。這代表若 10Y 重新上破 4.55%，selloff 可能被 mortgage rebalancing 放大；若 10Y 跌破 4.45%，同一機制也可能短線放大反彈。

美元與黃金的組合顯示市場尚未切換到全面避險。DXY 回落至 99.22、黃金升至 4,470 美元，但 S&P 500 與 Dow 同時上漲，表示投資人把油價回落與利率下行視為 risk broadening，而不是衰退避險。對 FI 來說，這種組合對 5Y-10Y 有利，卻不一定利多超長端，因為風險資產仍吸收資金。

因此本次定價邏輯應被描述為 mild 轉陡 with 偏黏著長端 premium。前端與中段受益於初領失業金與油價，長端受限於供給、MBS convexity、BOJ normalization 與 AI funding。若未來幾日 2s10s 只在 40-45bp 區間震盪，代表市場仍在等待非農與 6 月 FOMC；若 Curve 快速打開，才表示 Fed cut tail 或期限溢價有一方明顯改變。

三、其他市場動向跨市驗證顯示央行分化、亞洲 FX 防守、信用供給與 AI funding 同時重塑風險定價。

日本是全球央行分化的核心。實質薪資年增 1.9%、高於 1.7% 預期並連續第四個月為正，名目薪資 +3.5%、基本薪資 +3.4%，都支持 BOJ 6/15-16 升息 25bp 的討論；但 household spending 實質年減 0.5%，連五個月下降。這個組合讓 JGB 與日圓很難單向交易，因為 wage-price loop 變強，但家計需求仍限制 BOJ 追趕油價的速度。

日本政策還有財政與補貼層。政府準備 3.1 兆日圓追加預算，可能延長汽油與電力補貼；食品與飲料業者 6 月漲價產品超過 1,000 項，遠高於 5 月的 84 項。補貼可壓低 headline，但也延後相對價格調整，對 BOJ 而言反而增加判斷 underlying inflation 的難度；對美元債投資人而言，JPY 接近 160 仍是 FX / NAV 的核心風險。

澳洲與紐西蘭提供另一種 Duration 訊號。RBA 現金利率已回到 4.35%，Bullock 表示升息正在透過住房市場發揮作用，Q1 GDP 只增 0.3%、4 月 household spending -1.1%、失業率升至四年半高點；紐西蘭 2031 債標售 bid-to-cover 達 6.22，為該券 2020 年以來最高。這些市場顯示，若成長放緩與供給削減同時出現，中段 Duration 仍能吸引真實需求。

歐洲不是同步升息。Czech CPI 5 月 2.1%，低於 2.3% 預期且符合央行預測，服務價格 4.7% 仍高但降低立即升息壓力；Sweden CPIF ex-energy +0.5%，高於市場 0.3% 但低於 Riksbank 0.9% 預測，headline 1.5% 接近央行路徑。這代表歐洲 rates trade 會更依賴各國核心、服務與 FX，而不是只跟著 ECB 或油價走。

亞洲 FX 防守仍是區域資產的主線。韓元跌到 2009 年以來低點，印尼盾觸及紀錄低位，印度盧比在 95-96 區間、接近 97 的紀錄低點；韓國、印尼、菲律賓與印度都用口頭警告、干預或資本流入措施守匯率。這會讓本地債牛市被匯率風險打斷，也使美元走勢成為 TWD 海外債 ETF 的主要跨市風險。

信用一級市場仍能開啟，但需要清楚故事與保護。Schneider Electric 以 AI 資料中心 power / cooling narrative 發行 8 億歐元可轉債，票息 0.25%-0.75%、轉換溢價 35%-40%，並回購 6.5 億歐元 2030 年債；Coastal GasLink 準備 C$1bn 兩段式高評級債，短段約政府債 +100bp、長段 +115bp。這些例子支持 IG 與 infrastructure 收益，但不代表所有 credit beta 都可追。

AI funding 的供需兩面都在放大。Whale Rock 看好 Anthropic 使用者與 enterprise 市場，SpaceX 擬籌 750 億美元並可能以 1.77 兆美元估值上市；同時 DoubleLine 提醒 AI debt 最終幾乎一定會形成 bubble，去年以來相關高級、HY、貸款與其他 debt 銷售超過 3,700 億美元。對 FI 來說，AI 是盈利與資本支出支撐，也是長端供給與信用槓桿來源。

特殊信用風險更分散。Nassau County tobacco settlement bonds 未付 3,600 萬美元 principal，成為 800 億美元 tobacco sector 的首例 default，因 2025 settlement distributions 年減 19%、美國成人吸菸率降至 9.9%；Sleep Number 可能準備 bankruptcy loan，2025 revenue 14 億美元、年減 16%，股價四個月跌約 90%。這些不是宏觀 beta，而是收入池收縮與資本結構壓力。

EM 主權仍由條款、商品與資本流主導。Zambia 可能把 2053 bond 回購提高到 85c，債券約 82.14c，若 IMF 指標達成票息將由 0.5% 升至 7.5%；Chile 追加 62 億美元借款，另有 174 億美元原授權，2032 peso bond 標售比二級市場高 12bp，2056 高 10bp。投資人會買收益，但 fiscal credibility 與重整 mechanics 決定利差。

South Africa rand 收益顯示高 real 殖利率仍能吸引資金。Rand 自 4 月以來美元 funded 收益回報約 5%，非居民買入 372.9 億 rand 政府債，10Y 殖利率降 67bp 至約 8.65%；但 SARB 上周升息 25bp 至 7%，仍提示若油價衝突持續可能再緊。這說明 EM 收益可以恢復，但需要美元回落、vol 下降與政策可信度同時支撐。

四、配置含義配置可回補中段 Duration，但長端、HY beta 與亞洲未避險 NAV 仍需門檻。

Duration 建議從 short-to-neutral 調回 neutral-to-slight-long，但只集中在 5Y-10Y，不追 20Y-30Y。2Y 與 5Y 日降 3bp、初領失業金 225k 高於預期、WTI 跌近 3%，使中段有基本面回落；但 30Y 仍在 4.97%，IG 供給、MBS convexity 與 AI 資本支出仍在。實作上，10Y 4.45% 附近才分批加碼，4.55% 上方則停止追價。

Curve 部位偏好 mild 轉陡的防守表達，而不是 aggressively long 長端。2s10s 42bp、5s30s 79bp，日內各轉陡 1bp，但周/月仍大幅收斂，代表政策風險尚未完全退場。若非農弱於 +50k 且薪資低於 +0.2%，可把 5Y-10Y bull steepener 放大；若非農高於 +125k，應把前端回落視為賣點。

Credit / ETF 仍偏高品質收益。IG 一級市場達 1 兆美元且高評級 AI / infrastructure 名稱能清盤，支持 short/intermediate IG；但 tobacco default、Sleep Number bankruptcy risk、Reno de Medici 債券約 26c、software / 私募信貸 stress 都提醒 HY beta 不能用 index 利差一筆帶過。配置上保留 IG、金融、utility、data-center picks-and-shovels，避免弱零售與單一收入池結構。

EM 配置可做 selective 收益，但不應放大本幣匯率風險。South Africa 有 5% 收益回報與 372.9 億 rand 債券流入，Zambia 有 tender catalyst，Congo 受銅鈷與 AI 基礎設施 narrative 支撐；相對地 Chile fiscal slippage、Asia FX intervention 與印度盧比壓力仍會放大本地債波動。EM 硬通貨優先於未避險本幣，且要避開油價再上 98 美元的情境。

TWD 計價海外債 ETF 的加碼條件比昨日起更接近，但仍需確認。10Y 4.47% 已回到可分批區間，DXY 99.22 有助 NAV，但 USD/JPY 接近 160 與亞洲 FX 防守顯示匯率成本未完全釋放。若 DXY 跌破 99、WTI 低於 92、10Y 守在 4.45%-4.50%，可把加碼從觀察轉成執行；若 DXY 重新上 100，維持小步分批。

長端配置只適合用 convexity 小比例表達。若 30Y 上破 5.05%，MBS hedging、Treasury funding 與 AI debt crowding-out 會使 selloff 放大；若 30Y 跌破 4.90% 且 10Y 同步跌破 4.45%，才代表實錢資金 demand 重新接管。退休金或保險帳戶可以保留長端 convexity，但 total-return 帳戶應把主要風險放在 5Y-10Y。

現金與短票仍有配置價值。Fed path 雖被初領失業金降溫，但 Daly、Logan 與 Warsh 首次 FOMC 都要求保留再定價空間；若周五非農就業是 Goldilocks，短端收益會慢慢被再投資壓力吃掉，若數據太強，短票反而提供保護。因此 barbell 仍合理：短端留流動性，中段收反彈，長端小比例做 hedge。

五、後續觀察重點交易觸發集中在非農、10Y/30Y 關鍵位、WTI、DXY、BOJ 與信用供給。

第一個 trigger 是周五非農。若非農就業高於 +125k、失業率低於 4.3% 或 AHE 高於 +0.3% m/m，2Y 4.05% 的回落會很快消失，10Y 可能回測 4.55%；若非農就業低於 +50k、初領失業金四周均值續升，10Y 跌破 4.45% 的機率上升，5Y-10Y Duration 可以再加一段。

第二個 trigger 是 WTI 92 / 98。WTI 低於 92 美元且外交進展延續，前端通膨溢價會繼續下降；WTI 重新站上 98 美元，Fed 對 energy pass-through 的防守會回來，2s10s 可能再收斂。這也是 TWD 海外債 ETF 加碼或暫緩的直接門檻。

第三個 trigger 是 10Y 4.45% / 4.55% 與 30Y 5.05%。10Y 跌破 4.45% 且 30Y 不守在 5% 上方，代表反彈已從前端傳到長端；10Y 上破 4.55% 或 30Y 上破 5.05%，則說明非農就業、MBS hedge 或供給壓力重新主導，Duration 要降回中性。

第四個 trigger 是 DXY 99 / 100。DXY 低於 99，亞洲 FX 防守壓力會下降，TWD 計價海外債 NAV 更容易保留 UST 反彈；DXY 重新上 100，韓元、印尼盾、盧比與日圓都可能再承壓，未避險海外債與 EM 本幣債需同步降風險。

第五個 trigger 是 BOJ 6/15-16。若日本薪資資料促成 25bp hike 且 Ueda 提示 2026 還有加息空間，JGB 長端與避險成本會限制亞洲買美元債；若 BOJ 只小幅升息且強調 household spending 疲弱，日圓可能先反彈、JGB selloff 可控，對 USD Duration 反而是穩定因子。

第六個 trigger 是信用供給品質。若 IG 新券仍能縮窄、Schneider 類 AI 基礎設施 paper 被快速吸收、Coastal GasLink 高評級 supply 順利，credit 收益可保留；若 tobacco、retail、software 或私募信貸相關壓力擴散到新券 concessions，Credit / ETF beta 要比 Duration 更早降風險。

第七個 trigger 是 MBS convexity。若 10Y 接近 4.55% 時 MBS OAS 擴大、mortgage hedgers 增加 receiver-to-payer adjustment，selloff 可能被放大；若 10Y 回到 4.40%-4.45% 且 MBS 穩定，convexity flow 會反過來支撐反彈。這個 trigger 決定長端能否加入中段回落。

Investment Read

Rates Close

1. UST Close And Curve

USTs delivered front-end and belly relief, with the long end still sticky.

The Treasury close is best defined as front-end and belly relief, not a clean long-end bull flattening. The 2Y closed at 4.05%, down 3bp, the 5Y at 4.18%, down 3bp, the 10Y at 4.47%, down 2bp, and the 20Y/30Y at 4.98% and 4.97%. The 2s10s curve mildly steepened to 42bp and 5s30s to 79bp, showing that claims and lower oil were first priced through policy-sensitive sectors while long-end term premium stayed in place.

Official CMT weekly and monthly changes show that the rally only reversed part of the prior hike-premium move, not the broader high-rate backdrop. The 2Y is still 7bp higher on the week and 12bp higher on the month, while the 5Y is up 5bp and 10bp. The 10Y is up 2bp on the week and 4bp on the month, while the 30Y is 2bp lower on the week and 1bp lower on the month. That argues against treating one relief session as a full return to a Fed-cut path.

Initial claims were the first rates catalyst. Claims rose by 13k to 225k, above the 211k expectation and the 212k prior, the highest since February. The four-week average rose to 214.75k, while continuing claims slipped to 1.78mn. The data do not show uncontrolled layoffs, but they are enough to question a rate-hike narrative built only on ADP +122k and ISM Services at 54.5.

Oil was the second catalyst. WTI closed near $92.50, down about 2.9%, while Brent traded near $96.40 as investors priced better odds of diplomacy around US-Iran talks and the Israel-Lebanon ceasefire. A lower energy-risk premium directly reduces CPI pass-through and second-round Fed concerns, so the 2Y-5Y sector had more room to rally than the 30Y. Still, as long as Strait-related uncertainty remains, a 10Y break below 4.45% is not a risk-free signal.

Cross-assets confirmed the interpretation. The S&P 500 rose 0.4% to 7,584.31, the Dow rose 1.7% to a record 51,561.93, and the Nasdaq slipped 0.1% to 26,830.96 as investors rotated away from chips toward financials, small caps and cyclicals. Stronger equity breadth alongside a UST rally points to lower oil and front-end policy relief, not a recessionary flight to quality.

The long end remains sticky. The 20Y closed at 4.98%, the 30Y at 4.97%, and 10s30s stayed at 50bp, meaning the long end did not match the 2Y and 5Y move. MBS convexity, AI capital spending, IG supply and fiscal deficits remain long-end constraints. For the 10Y to break 4.45% cleanly, payrolls and wages need to confirm slower demand, not just one claims print.

Supply and market structure are becoming part of the rates read. US IG issuance has already reached $1tn, the fastest pace since 2020, and Apollo expects full-year IG sales may exceed net Treasury issuance. At the same time, more than $2tn of 5%+ coupon MBS outstanding and roughly one-third of MBS near par mean the 30Y can become more sensitive in selloffs. These are not always daily drivers, but they cap the durability of curve relief.

Rates Interpretation

2. Rates Interpretation

The market moved from overheated hike-risk pricing to a two-way Fed path awaiting payrolls.

The rally cooled year-end hike risk; it did not make the Fed dovish. Daly said policy is well placed but must stay ready to respond either way, Logan kept 2026 hike risk alive, and Morgan Stanley argued that the Fed may look through war-driven oil as a growth shock while focusing on core inflation, fiscal policy, bank loan growth and AI spending. The front end moved from one-way hawkish repricing back into a data-dependent range.

Claims at 225k immediately raised the stakes for Friday payrolls. If payrolls land near the +85k consensus, unemployment stays at 4.3%, and average hourly earnings print +0.3% m/m, the 2Y around 4.05% can hold. If hiring exceeds +125k or wages reaccelerate, the 2Y-5Y relief will be repriced quickly. The curve should therefore be described as payrolls-before-risk-reduction, not durable bull steepening.

Oil transmission is now more nuanced. A near-3% WTI drop reduces the immediate inflation premium, but the Fed still faces price gauges around 3.8%, energy costs above pre-war levels, and delayed pass-through into freight, packaging and transport. As long as crude stays above $90, officials cannot treat the whole shock as a one-off supply event. Front-end yields can rally, but 2026 hike optionality should not be set to zero.

Long-end pressure is more about funding than a single macro datapoint. Apollo highlighted that AI, defense, utilities and data centers require large pools of long-duration capital, while US IG supply has reached $1tn. Schneider used the AI data-center story to issue EUR800mn of convertible bonds and refinance 2030 debt. Absolute 30Y yields near 4.97% attract buyers, but term premium is unlikely to compress quickly while corporate and Treasury funding needs are elevated.

MBS convexity can amplify long-end volatility. A larger high-coupon MBS universe closer to par requires more frequent hedge adjustment, and Goldman estimated that the recent selloff changed active mortgage hedgers' rate exposure by an amount equivalent to $40bn of 10Y Treasuries. If 10Y breaks back above 4.55%, mortgage rebalancing can worsen the selloff; if it breaks below 4.45%, the same mechanism can amplify a rally.

The dollar and gold mix shows that markets have not moved into full defensive mode. DXY slipped to 99.22 and gold rose to $4,470, but the S&P 500 and Dow also rallied. Investors treated lower oil and lower yields as risk broadening, not as a recession hedge. That supports 5Y-10Y duration, but it is not necessarily bullish for the ultra-long end because risk assets are still absorbing capital.

The session is therefore mild steepening with sticky long-end premium. The front end and belly benefited from claims and oil, while the long end is constrained by supply, MBS convexity, BOJ normalization and AI funding. If 2s10s only ranges between 40bp and 45bp over the next few sessions, the market is still waiting for payrolls and the June FOMC. A faster curve break would require either a clear Fed-cut tail or a meaningful term-premium shift.

Cross-Market Read

3. Cross-Market Read

Central-bank divergence, Asian FX defense, credit supply and AI funding are all reshaping risk pricing.

Japan is the center of global central-bank divergence. Real wages rose 1.9%, beating 1.7% expectations and marking a fourth positive month, while nominal wages rose 3.5% and base pay 3.4%. These numbers support a June 15-16 BOJ hike debate. But real household spending fell 0.5%, the fifth monthly decline, so JGBs and the yen are not a one-way trade: the wage-price loop is stronger, but household demand limits how aggressively the BOJ can chase oil.

Japanese policy also has a fiscal and subsidy layer. The government is preparing a JPY3.1tn extra budget and may extend gasoline and electricity subsidies. Food and beverage companies plan to raise prices on more than 1,000 products in June, versus 84 in May. Subsidies can restrain headline inflation while delaying relative-price adjustment, which complicates the BOJ's read of underlying inflation. For dollar-bond investors, USD/JPY near 160 remains the key FX/NAV risk.

Australia and New Zealand offer a different duration signal. The RBA cash rate is back at 4.35%, Bullock said hikes are working through housing, Q1 GDP rose only 0.3%, April household spending fell 1.1%, and unemployment reached a four-and-a-half-year high. New Zealand's 2031 auction drew a 6.22 bid-to-cover, the highest since that bond was first sold in 2020. Where slower growth meets lower supply, belly duration can still attract real demand.

Europe is not a synchronized hiking block. Czech CPI slowed to 2.1%, below the 2.3% estimate and in line with the central bank's forecast, while services inflation at 4.7% is still high but reduces imminent-hike pressure. Sweden's CPIF ex-energy rose 0.5%, above the 0.3% market estimate but below the Riksbank's 0.9% projection, with headline CPIF at 1.5%. European rates trades will depend on local core, services and FX stability, not simply on ECB or oil direction.

Asian FX defense remains a regional asset theme. The won fell to its weakest level since 2009, the rupiah hit a record low, and the rupee traded around 95-96, near the 97 record level. Korea, Indonesia, the Philippines and India are using warnings, intervention or capital-inflow measures to defend exchange rates. That can interrupt local-bond rallies and makes dollar direction the main cross-market risk for TWD overseas-bond ETFs.

Credit primary markets remain open when the story and protections are clear. Schneider issued EUR800mn of convertible bonds around an AI data-center power and cooling narrative, with a 0.25%-0.75% coupon and 35%-40% conversion premium, while repurchasing EUR650mn of 2030 debt. Coastal GasLink is preparing C$1bn of high-grade two-part supply around government +100bp and +115bp. The message supports IG and infrastructure carry, not indiscriminate credit beta.

AI funding has two sides. Whale Rock is constructive on Anthropic users and enterprise adoption, and SpaceX is seeking to raise $75bn at a possible $1.77tn valuation. At the same time, DoubleLine warned that AI debt will almost certainly become a bubble, with more than $370bn of related IG, HY, loan and other debt supply since the start of last year. For FI, AI is both an earnings/capex support and a source of long-end supply and credit leverage.

Idiosyncratic credit risk is spreading. Nassau County tobacco settlement bonds missed a $36mn principal payment, the first default in the $80bn tobacco sector, after 2025 settlement distributions fell 19% and US adult smoking dropped to 9.9%. Sleep Number is preparing possible bankruptcy financing after 2025 revenue fell 16% to $1.4bn and its stock dropped about 90% in four months. These are not macro beta stories; they are shrinking revenue pools and capital-structure stress.

EM sovereigns remain driven by terms, commodities and capital flows. Zambia may lift the 2053 buyback offer to 85c, with the bond near 82.14c, while the coupon could step from 0.5% to 7.5% if IMF metrics are met. Chile is seeking $6.2bn of additional borrowing on top of $17.4bn of authority, and recent 2032 and 2056 issuance cleared 12bp and 10bp above secondary levels. Investors will buy yield, but fiscal credibility and restructuring mechanics determine spreads.

Stronger equity breadth alongside a UST rally points to lower oil and front-end policy relief, not a recessionary flight to quality.

MBS convexity, AI capital spending, IG supply and fiscal deficits remain long-end constraints. For the 10Y to break 4.45% cleanly, payrolls and wages need to confirm slower demand, not just one claims print.

2. Rates InterpretationThe market moved from overheated hike-risk pricing to a two-way Fed path awaiting payrolls.

The rally cooled year-end hike risk; it did not make the Fed dovish. Daly said policy is well placed but must stay ready to respond either way, Logan kept 2026 hike risk alive, and Morgan Stanley argued that the Fed may look through war-driven oil as a growth shock while focusing on core inflation, fiscal policy, bank loan growth and AI spending. The front end moved from one-way hawkish repricing back into a data-dependent range.

Claims at 225k immediately raised the stakes for Friday payrolls. If payrolls land near the +85k consensus, unemployment stays at 4.3%, and average hourly earnings print +0.3% m/m, the 2Y around 4.05% can hold. If hiring exceeds +125k or wages reaccelerate, the 2Y-5Y relief will be repriced quickly. The curve should therefore be described as payrolls-before-risk-reduction, not durable bull steepening.

Oil transmission is now more nuanced. A near-3% WTI drop reduces the immediate inflation premium, but the Fed still faces price gauges around 3.8%, energy costs above pre-war levels, and delayed pass-through into freight, packaging and transport. As long as crude stays above $90, officials cannot treat the whole shock as a one-off supply event. Front-end yields can rally, but 2026 hike optionality should not be set to zero.

Long-end pressure is more about funding than a single macro datapoint. Apollo highlighted that AI, defense, utilities and data centers require large pools of long-duration capital, while US IG supply has reached $1tn. Schneider used the AI data-center story to issue EUR800mn of convertible bonds and refinance 2030 debt. Absolute 30Y yields near 4.97% attract buyers, but term premium is unlikely to compress quickly while corporate and Treasury funding needs are elevated.

MBS convexity can amplify long-end volatility. A larger high-coupon MBS universe closer to par requires more frequent hedge adjustment, and Goldman estimated that the recent selloff changed active mortgage hedgers' rate exposure by an amount equivalent to $40bn of 10Y Treasuries. If 10Y breaks back above 4.55%, mortgage rebalancing can worsen the selloff; if it breaks below 4.45%, the same mechanism can amplify a rally.

The dollar and gold mix shows that markets have not moved into full defensive mode. DXY slipped to 99.22 and gold rose to $4,470, but the S&P 500 and Dow also rallied. Investors treated lower oil and lower yields as risk broadening, not as a recession hedge. That supports 5Y-10Y duration, but it is not necessarily bullish for the ultra-long end because risk assets are still absorbing capital.

The session is therefore mild steepening with sticky long-end premium. The front end and belly benefited from claims and oil, while the long end is constrained by supply, MBS convexity, BOJ normalization and AI funding. If 2s10s only ranges between 40bp and 45bp over the next few sessions, the market is still waiting for payrolls and the June FOMC. A faster curve break would require either a clear Fed-cut tail or a meaningful term-premium shift.

Japan is the center of global central-bank divergence. Real wages rose 1.9%, beating 1.7% expectations and marking a fourth positive month, while nominal wages rose 3.5% and base pay 3.4%. These numbers support a June 15-16 BOJ hike debate. But real household spending fell 0.5%, the fifth monthly decline, so JGBs and the yen are not a one-way trade: the wage-price loop is stronger, but household demand limits how aggressively the BOJ can chase oil.

Europe is not a synchronized hiking block. European rates trades will depend on local core, services and FX stability, not simply on ECB or oil direction.

Asian FX defense remains a regional asset theme. The won fell to its weakest level since 2009, the rupiah hit a record low, and the rupee traded around 95-96, near the 97 record level. Korea, Indonesia, the Philippines and India are using warnings, intervention or capital-inflow measures to defend exchange rates. That can interrupt local-bond rallies and makes dollar direction the main cross-market risk for TWD overseas-bond ETFs.

Credit primary markets remain open when the story and protections are clear. At the same time, DoubleLine warned that AI debt will almost certainly become a bubble, with more than $370bn of related IG, HY, loan and other debt supply since the start of last year. For FI, AI is both an earnings/capex support and a source of long-end supply and credit leverage.
